WHAT IS TOUGH GLOVE?

Tough Glove is dedicated to teaching the sweet science of boxing while offering the benefits of a full body HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training) workout in one seamless class. Our classes are strictly non contact.

Our aim is to provide a unique and enjoyable experience to everyone who takes to the glove, regardless of ability and fitness level.

Just like tough love, our class will challenge participants and bring them to the best of their ability.

WHY TOUGH GLOVE?

Unlike most boxing-based fitness classes, our classes are lead and instructed by passionate and active students of the game. Our instructors have experienced the training and preparations that are endured when competing at an elite level, bringing this wisdom to each class. Where better to learn than from the pugilists themselves?

Our classes have been designed using a training system that incorporates the perfect balance of both physical fitness and technical skills giving participants the best bang for their buck.

Community and inclusivity is at the heart of what we do.

MEET YOUR INSTRUCTORS

TONY BROWNE

Tony Browne, our Tough Glove head trainer, is currently a professional boxer after having a very successful amateur career winning national titles and competing and training full time with the national team for many years.

Tony has travelled the world with boxing and been involved in training camps with some of the greatest boxers and trainers from around the globe.

This wealth of experience combined with his certification in personal physical training and his Degree in health and performance science from UCD brings him an edge when it comes to teaching these classes.

TONY

DYLAN MURPHY

Dylan Murphy is a competitive amateur boxer with Portlaoise Boxing Club and qualified personal trainer through Elite Academy, Blackrock. Dylan graduated with a first-class honours degree and soon found himself pursuing his passion in health, fitness and well-being.

A late starter in boxing, Dylan first walked into a boxing gym at 21 years old. He has competed at Novice, Intermediate and International Elite level.

After winning an All Ireland university title in his final year of college, he hopes to add to that success over the coming years.

DYLAN
